The Maker

Noa Shechter

Noa works out of a small studio on Nahalat Binyamin, Tel Aviv. Each piece begins with raw material — sterling silver 925, solid brass, copper, or 14k gold — and is shaped entirely by hand.

There are no moulds, no mass production. Every ring, cuff, and pendant carries the trace of the maker: a slight asymmetry, a hand-hammered surface, a finish that deepens with time.

The work is unisex by intention. Good jewelry should belong to the person wearing it — not to a category.

Sterling Silver 925

Hard-wearing, bright, and oxidizes beautifully over time. The studio workhorse — used in most daily-wear and stacking pieces.

02

Solid Brass

Warm, heavy, and ages into a rich patina. Frequently paired with silver for contrast — a metal that tells stories.

03

14k Gold

Reserved for heirloom-grade commissions. Pure, lasting, and made entirely to order with extended lead times.
