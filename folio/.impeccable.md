# Noa — theme design context

## Design Context

### Users
Shoppers browsing handmade unisex jewelry (rings, earrings, bracelets, necklaces) from Noa Shechter's studio on Nahalat Binyamin, Tel Aviv. Browsing on desktop and mobile, often discovery-led (lifestyle imagery, hotspots) rather than search-led.

### Brand Personality
Warm · handmade · quietly confident. Artisan, not mass-produced; mixed metals (sterling silver, brass, copper, 14k gold), one-of-a-kind pieces. Editorial and calm, never loud or salesy.

### Aesthetic Direction
Warm-minimal editorial. Light theme, parchment/stone neutrals, ink `#1c1c1c`, warm gold accents. Generous whitespace, large tactile product photography, restraint over decoration.
- **Display font:** Cormorant Garamond (established brand serif — keep; headings are set uppercase with open tracking site-wide).
- **Body font:** Instrument Sans.
- Anti-references: neon/tech gradients, glassmorphism, drop-shadow card grids, anything that reads "AI template."

### Design Principles
1. **Photography leads.** Product/lifestyle imagery is the hero; type supports it.
2. **Warm neutral palette.** Tint neutrals toward parchment/gold; never pure black/white.
3. **Editorial restraint.** Fewer, larger type steps with real contrast; generous, varied spacing.
4. **Legible over imagery.** Light text on photos always gets a controlled gradient/overlay for contrast.
5. **Cohesive with existing tokens** (`--fs-hero`, `--gutter`, color vars) — extend, don't reinvent.

_Note: Cormorant Garamond + Instrument Sans are the locked brand fonts. The impeccable "reflex font" ban is overridden here by established brand identity._
