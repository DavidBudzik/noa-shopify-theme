# Noa Unisex Jewelry — Brand Book Project Brief

## Project overview

Create a complete brand identity guidelines document (brand book) for **Noa Unisex Jewelry**, a Tel Aviv–based handmade jewelry studio. The deliverable is a polished, single-file HTML document that can be opened in any browser and shared digitally.

---

## About the brand

**Studio name:** Noa Unisex Jewelry (also: NOArtJewelry, Noa Shechter)  
**Designer:** Noa Shechter  
**Location:** Tel Aviv, Israel  
**Sales channels:** Nahalat Binyamin Art Market (Tuesdays & Fridays), Etsy shop (noartjewelry.etsy.com), Instagram (@noa.unisexjewelry)

**What the brand is:**  
A one-person metalsmith and goldsmith studio producing handmade unisex jewelry. Every piece is made by hand in Noa's home studio. The work spans silver and gold, using traditional goldsmithing and metalsmithing techniques. The brand philosophy: "Less is more. Earthy, natural, modern, smart designs with clean cuts. Made for women and men as one."

**Brand values:** Earthy · Unisex · Artisan · Modern · Wearable · One-of-a-kind

## Brand voice

**Tone:** Quiet confidence. Earthy honesty. Warm and personal. Never loud, never gendered.

**Do write:**
- "Hammered from solid 14k gold. Fits like it was always there."
- "Wear it on whichever finger feels right. Made for everyone."
- "Handmade in Tel Aviv."

**Never write:**
- Exclamation marks, superlatives, ALL CAPS for emphasis
- Gendered language ("for him", "the lady in your life")
- Over-romanticized product names

**Key phrases:** "Handmade in Tel Aviv" · "Designed for everyone" · "One of a kind" · "Made to be worn every day"

## Photography style

1. **Natural light only** — window light or open shade, never flash
2. **Warm neutral backgrounds** — linen, raw concrete, aged wood, bare skin
3. **On-body priority** — show pieces worn; mix product-only and on-body 1:2
4. **Process documentation** — tools, sketches, the market stall
5. **Shallow depth of field** — focus on the piece, surroundings soft
6. **Unisex casting** — feature all genders and expressions
7. **Post-processing** — minimal; warm midtones; never cool or over-saturate

---

## Brand book structure (7 sections)

The HTML document should include these sections in order, with a fixed navigation bar:

1. **Brand Story** — origin, philosophy, values grid
2. **Logo & Visual Identity** — mark description, 3 colourway examples, do/don't rules
3. **Color Palette** — 8 swatches with hex codes and usage notes
4. **Typography** — type specimen showing all 5 roles
5. **Brand Voice & Messaging** — 4 voice pillars, do/don't copy examples
6. **Photography Style** — 6 principles, post-processing note
7. **Product Collections** — 9 collection cards, naming convention

---

## Design direction for the HTML file

- **Aesthetic:** Refined editorial minimalism. Luxury without loudness.
- **Layout:** Max 760px content width, centered. Massive white space. 7–8rem vertical padding per section.
- **Background:** Pure white `#ffffff` throughout. No colored backgrounds except within demo blocks.
- **Borders:** `1px solid #f0ece6` (very subtle warm rule). Never black borders.
- **Dividers:** A 32px wide, 1px tall gold line (`#d4bc96`) used sparingly between sub-sections.
- **Navigation:** Fixed top bar, 56px tall, white with blur backdrop, gold accent on hover.
- **Pull quotes:** Playfair Display italic, 1.55rem, `#b89a6a` gold color.
- **Section tags:** 10px Outfit ExtraLight, 0.45em letter-spacing, uppercase, gold color.
- **Values/collection grids:** Hairline border grid — `1px solid #f0ece6` — no background fill.
- **Do/Don't blocks:** Green-left-border on cream-green for "do", terracotta-left-border on cream-pink for "don't".
- **Responsive:** Works on mobile (single column below 640px).
- **Self-contained:** All SVG logo paths embedded inline. Google Fonts loaded via `<link>`. No external dependencies beyond fonts.
