Care & storage+
Store in a dry place away from moisture. Polish occasionally with a soft cloth to lift the high points. The oxidized finish will deepen naturally — this is part of the piece's character, not a flaw.
Ring sizing guide+
Measure your finger at its widest point. Ring sizes run true. If between sizes, size up. Custom sizing available — contact the studio before ordering.
